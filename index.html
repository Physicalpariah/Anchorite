COMING SOON TO iOS

Unstoppable is a hardcore post-apocalyptic truck-diving sim for iOS with procedurally generated levels. You must make it to the other side of a snowy gauntlet filled with raiders hell-bent on wiping you out. At your disposal are machine guns, mounted spikes, circular saws, flamethrowers, miniguns and minions to turn your rig into an unstoppable death-machine!
